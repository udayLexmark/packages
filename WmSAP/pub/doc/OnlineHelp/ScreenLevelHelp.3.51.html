Forward Confirm Event
All synchronous adapter notifications (the RFC listener notification, ALE listener notification, and the routing notification) support the Forward Confirm Event feature.
If On, the Forward Confirm Event flag is activated and the SAP Adapter tries to forward the Confirm TID event, which is triggered by the tRFC protocol, to the same destination where the document was sent. What happens then depends on the transport used.
